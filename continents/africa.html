Literacy Rates Throughout Africa

Africa has some of the lowest literacy rates throughout the world. However, not all of africais suffering from low literacy rates. Southern Africa has an average of 80.3% literacy rate, and North and East Africa present similar rates at 71.8% and 71.3%, respectfully. Central and West Africa are struggling more at 67.5% and 54.1%.
